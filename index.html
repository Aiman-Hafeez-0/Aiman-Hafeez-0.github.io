About

Where engineering meets analysis.

I'm Aiman Hafeez, a Computer Science student at Begum Nusrat Bhutto Women University, Sukkur, graduating in 2026 with a 3.89/4.0 CGPA. My work sits at the intersection of production software and applied machine learning — I've shipped real-time features on live codebases and built models that classify financial risk from raw statements.

At Systems Limited, Pakistan's leading software house, I built JWT-authenticated REST APIs and a Socket.IO real-time messaging layer supporting concurrent sessions. In parallel, my Final Year Project applies supervised and unsupervised learning to forecast liquidity risk and classify PSX-listed firms.

Most recently, as an AI/ML Research Intern at Pulse, I've built physics-constrained demand forecasting models, pushing accuracy from ~11% to ~2.2% MAPE with 0% physical violation rate, while fixing pipeline data leakage and improving regime gating across 5 US RTOs, the kind of validation rigor I carry into every model I ship..
